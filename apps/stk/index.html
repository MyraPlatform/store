SuperTuxKart

By: SuperTuxKart Team (ported by The Myra Project)

Version: 1.4.0

SuperTuxKart is a free and open-source kart racing game featuring Tux (the Linux mascot) and friends. Race through a variety of tracks, use power-ups, and try to become the best driver in the world! Great for kids, open-source fans, and anyone who enjoys fun, arcade-style racing.
